The Ōtori-Sama (オオトリ様, Ōtorisama, lit. "Lord Big Birds") are minor characters in the film Spirited Away directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli. They are spirits resembling giant chickens or ducks that are clients of the Bathhouse, here they put themselves into bath tubs.
